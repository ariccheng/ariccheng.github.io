Service Operations Last-Mile Delivery Online Platform Causal Inference Applied Econometrics

My research examines platform-enabled service operations, focusing on how information quality and coordination mechanisms shape frontline productivity and performance. I pursue this agenda in two streams: (i) service operations in last-mile delivery, and (ii) operations and governance in crowdsourcing platforms, with an emphasis on volunteer engagement in online communities. My work leverages large-scale operational and geospatial data and uses rigorous empirical identification to generate credible managerial and policy implications for firms and platforms.
