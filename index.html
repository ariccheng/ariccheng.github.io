Aric Cheng

👋🏼 Greetings! I'm Aric Cheng (程宇清), a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the NASPO Department of Supply Chain Management at W. P. Carey School of Business, Arizona State University. I am fortunate to be advised by Avnet Professor Elliot Rabinovich. I earned my Master's degree in Business Analytics from Washington University in 2023 and my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics in 2021.

Service Operations Last-Mile Delivery Online Platform Causal Inference Applied Econometrics

I study platform operations in the last mile, examining how digital platforms — from crowdsourced mapping communities to algorithmic dispatching systems — shape the efficiency and quality of physical delivery operations. My work combines large-scale archival data with econometric identification strategies to generate credible managerial insights.
